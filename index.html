Gamer Girl

A Harassment Simulator

Disaster!

You're the only girl in an all guy programming class. With creeps and weirdos around every corner, you better watch your every step like your academic career (and safety) depends on it!

Navigate through the world of a second year female game design and development student who faces threats and stalkers around every corner.

With stories taken from real cases of harassment and discrimination and character portraits taken from registered sex offenders, players are forced to 'woman up' and deal with their fearsome classmates.

Based On True Stories!

Taken from the testimonies and interviews of women majoring in Game Design and Development at the Rochester Institute of Technology, all in-game events are extracted from real life occurences.

While specific information has been altered to protect the identities of the interviewees, the stalking, angry outbursts, threats of violence, and manipulation are all taken from actual stories of abuse.
